The Value of the Web

How the web excels at furthering
the PBS mission

The Web is a Place of Equity

Our website is the most open and democratic way for the U.S. citizenry to reach our content.
The web is accessible by default.
Accessibility is designed into the HTML spec.
It’s up to us to make sure we don’t mess it up.

The Web is a Place of Freedom

Our users have the most freedom of getting to our content.
All you need is a browser. No specific device needed.
PBS is free to act in the way that is best for its users and our member stations
The web is our proving ground and where most features are First to Market.
  • CDM - Core Data Model
  • Franchises
  • GA Livestream
  • Continuous Play
  • Topics
I have the most freedom of how I want to build things
No one company controls the raw materials of the web - HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Some implementations may be more controlled, but they are changeable
Example: moving from JW Player to Video.js

The Web is a Place of Resilience

No other platform allows for such progressive enhancement
allowing all users a core experience, while more and more capable browsers get better and better experiences
